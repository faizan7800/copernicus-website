FAQs

What is a Fractional CTO?

A fractional CTO is a part-time or project-based Chief Technology Officer who provides strategic tech leadership without the cost of a full-time hire. They guide your technology roadmap, manage teams, and ensure tech aligns with business goals. Ideal for startups or growing companies, a fractional CTO offers flexible, experts overshight to help drive innovation and support growth.

How does a fractional CTO or CPO work with our team?

Fractional CTOs and CPOs integrate with your team, providing hands-on leadership while collaborating with your in-house staff. They offer strategic guidance, help strucutre processes, mentor teams memebers, and ensure alignment across product and technology inititives.
